About

Established in 2001, Koop King Multipurpose Cooperative serves active and retired personnel of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, Philippine Coast Guard, Bureau of Fire Protection, and Bureau of Jail Management and Penology. Its membership structure produces governance defined by institutional discipline and operational accountability.

In 2020, the cooperative achieved Billionaire Cooperative classification — a threshold reflecting sustained asset growth and financial maturity. It participates in the Koop Kapatid program as a mentor cooperative, extending operational experience into the broader ecosystem.

Demand consistently exceeds internal supply capacity, creating active opportunities for subcontracting and coordinated sourcing within the cooperative network.

Cargo movement and institutional shuttle services. Internal transport assets reduce third-party logistics dependency.

Procurement Demand

Consistent inbound demand for textiles, raw materials, printing inputs, fuel, and maintenance goods.
