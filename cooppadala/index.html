Coverage Rules

Same City / Province as Origin means a local movement where the destination is in the same city or province as the origin.

Special Area / Island covers Palawan, island destinations, or other special-area movements where special handling may apply.

Fragile, perishable, regulated, high-value, or unusual items should be cleared first before payment and forwarding.
